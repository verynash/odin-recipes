Pretzel Chicken

Discovered at a hospital when I was 12, pretzel chicken has become one of my specialty dishes. See below for a tasty new staple in your household.

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • pretzels (sticks or regular is fine)
  • vegetable oil
  • butter
  • flour
  • paprika
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayanne pepper
  • parsley leaves
  • red onion
  • eggs
  • milk
  • cheese
  • mustard

Steps

  1. Butterflly the chicken and flatten with a mallet to about 1/4" thickness.
  2. Grind pretzels in a food processor until fine. Combine in a shallow dish with paprika and season with salt and pepper. In another shallow dish, beat the eggs with a splash of water.
  3. Coat the pieces of chicken in the egg, and then in the ground pretzels.
  4. Heat a large skillet with 1/4" vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F.
  5. In a saucepan, melt butter. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute. Whisk in millk and cook until slightly thickened, about 3-5 minutes.
  6. Stir in cheese, mustard, paprika, cayanne, salt and pepper.
  7. Serve chicken with a drizzle of cheddar-mustard sauce and garnish with parsley and red onion.
