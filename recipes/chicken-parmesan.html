Chicken Parmesan

A cheap and easy meal, this can end up being one of the best meals of your week! Fried chicken cutlets, marinara, cheese? What could be better?

Ingredients

  • chicken breasts
  • eggs
  • buttermilk
  • garlic powder
  • kosher salt
  • pepper
  • flour
  • cornstarch
  • oregano
  • cayanne pepper
  • panko bread crumbs
  • vegetable oil
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • petite diced tomatoes
  • dried basil
  • fresh basil (optional)
  • spaghetti
  • parmesan cheese
  • mozzerella cheese

Steps

  1. Mix spices, flour, and cornstarch to create a dredging station. Beat eggs and mix with buttermilk.
  2. Marinate the chicken in the egg mixture, then coat in the dredging. Dip back into the egg mixture, and then coat it in panko bread crumbs.
  3. Fry chicken at 400 degrees F. Ensure the oil is back at 400 degrees F between batches of chicken.
  4. While the chicken is frying, simmer your sauce and boil your noodles in separate pots.
  5. Place the fried chicken on a baking sheet, top with marinara sauce and your cheeses, and broil in the oven.
  6. Serve hot! Mmm, delicious.
